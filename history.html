Since 1995 Primal Gym has been open to the public in order to help individuals channel their wild side. Primal gym has risen in popularity due to the unhealthy lifestyles of Americans and continues to provide fun and engaging ways to exercise. It is one of the few gyms that has a archery range as well as obstacle courses. Primal Gym welcomes all no matter your personal goals if its building muscle or just maintaining a healthy weight.
