Company History

Humans have evolved to be hunters and gatherers however today’s sedentary lifestyle has taken physical activities out of our lives and has affected our health Come to Primal Gym to stay active like our ancestors were. Here you can run, jump, skip, shoot arrows or throw a spear. Don’t know how to shoot an arrow or throw a spear? No problem! We offer archery and spear throwing lessons!
Do you just want to lift weights or just get a good run in? We have what you’re looking for! Anything to take back your wild side and reap the health benefits!
